Cassie Peele

I'm interested in nearly all aspects of the game development process, but my primary focus is the programming of gameplay and game mechanics. I love the satisfaction of watching multiple parts I've created work together to form a completed whole.

My dream job in the gamedev industry is that of an independent game developer, working either individually or in a small team. I prefer the feeling of working in a close-knit community to that of working in a large, impersonal studio.

Outside of school, I do minor game and webpage development, primarily for fun and experience. I have also studied the basics of multiple scripting languages (such as Python and JavaScript) on my own so that I can quickly test ideas I may have for larger projects.
